ZUT SRC Elections: Candidates Debate Student Welfare

Updated 2 hours ago | By Me TV Team

The race for the Student Representative Council presidency heats up as candidates faced off in the Great Hall today. Key topics included tuition fees, accommodation, and campus Wi-Fi improvements.

Incumbent president highlighted achievements in cafeteria subsidies, while the opposition promised a complete overhaul of the hostel allocation system. Voting begins next Monday at the Student Centre. Students are urged to bring their IDs.

Campus Updates

Hostel Renovations: The administration has announced that Block C will undergo renovations starting next month. Students affected will be temporarily reassigned.

Cultural Night: The annual International Cultural Night is set for Friday. Tickets are available at the Student Union building.

Academics & Innovation

Exam Schedule Released: The final exam timetable for the semester has been published on the student portal. Please check for any clashes.



ZUT Tech Fair: Computer Science students showcased a new AI-driven attendance system at the annual ZUT Tech Fair yesterday.

ZUT Sports

  • Football: ZUT Warriors defeat UNZA in a friendly match 2-1.
  • Basketball: Inter-hostel basketball tournament finals set for Saturday at the Sports Complex.
  • Athletics: ZUT track team qualifies for the National University Games.
