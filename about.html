About Me TV - ZUT

Me TV is the official multimedia and news station of the Zambia University College of Technology (ZUT). We are dedicated to reporting on student matters, academic updates, and campus life, ensuring that the university community stays informed and connected.

Our Mission

To serve the ZUT student body and faculty by providing timely, accurate, and relevant information. We aim to amplify the student voice, bridge the gap between administration and students, and highlight the achievements of our academic community.

Our Team

Me TV is powered by a dedicated team of student journalists, editors, and producers from within ZUT. Our passion is to cover stories that matter to you.
